Custom Name Entity Recognizer with spaCy
Domain: WTA Tournament Press Conferences

When I was a conference interpreter before the pandemic, one of the time-consuming tasks for me and many of my colleagues was (and still is I believe) to get familiarized with specific domains.

I'm trying to train a custom name entity model with spaCy for specific domains to help Chinese/English interpreters prepare for work.

That brings two problems:

  1. We cannot skip anything because we don't know where ideas new to us are
  2. It's difficult to find answers without Google (in China)

I hope a name entity recognizer with spaCy can help address these problems.

I choose the domain of WTA tournament press conferences because I did a lot of them and I personally like tennis very much.

High School Graduation Rates Prediction
What Factors Play A Big Role in Graduation Rates

A group project with Anel Akiyanova and Luke Heeringa

The purpose of this work was to determine what factors are most impactful in predicting high school graduation rates.

Both fiscal and non-fiscal factors were considered and we saw important ones in both categories. Eight fiscal factors were identified as strong predictors for graduation rates for now:

  1. Total Federal Revenue
  2. Total State Revenue
  3. Local Revenue - District Activity
  4. Teacher Salaries - support services - instructional staff
  5. Salaries - School Admin
  6. Salaries - business/ other
  7. Employee benefits - support services - school admin
  8. School Funds

Data source: https://nces.ed.gov/
